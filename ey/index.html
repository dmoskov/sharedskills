The Deception

Bruce Kent is not a superhero. He's an ordinary man who volunteered to pretend to be the legendary Masculine Mongoose to protect the real hero's identity.

The actual Mongoose, Baibhav Hegadi, has a large family—spouse, 3 kids, siblings, and 16 nieces/nephews—who would become targets if his identity were known.

So Bruce lives a lie that everyone "knows" but can't prove, facing assassination attempts, public scorn, and complete isolation—all without any powers to protect himself.

"I'm not a real hero. I'm just doing a job."
- Bruce Kent
