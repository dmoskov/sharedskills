A Typical Bruce Situation - Timeline

Scenario: Bank Robbery in Progress

Event Types

Critical Decision
Public Pressure
Internal Conflict
Villain Interaction
Tension: HIGH
2:47 PM
Bruce Enters the Bank
Bruce Kent walks into First National Bank to make a routine deposit. He's wearing his usual expensive suit, trying to maintain his playboy persona.
💭 "Just a normal day. Act natural. You're just Bruce Kent, millionaire playboy."
Tension: EXTREME
2:52 PM
Armed Robbers Storm In
Three masked criminals burst through the doors. One of them immediately recognizes Bruce and smirks. "Well, well, look who's here. The 'ordinary citizen' Bruce Kent."
💭 "They know. Of course they know. Everyone knows. Stay calm. Don't react."
Tension: CRITICAL
2:55 PM
Hostages Look to Bruce
Other bank customers start whispering and looking at Bruce expectantly. A mother shields her child while glancing hopefully at him. Everyone's waiting for the Masculine Mongoose to act.
💭 "They're all expecting me to save them. But I can't. I have no powers. I'm just Bruce."
Tension: EMOTIONAL
2:58 PM
The Child Questions
A young boy tugs on Bruce's sleeve. "Why aren't you stopping them? You're the Mongoose, right? My dad says you're the strongest hero ever."
💭 "How do I explain to a child that their hero is a lie? That I'm letting this happen?"
Tension: DANGEROUS
3:02 PM
Criminal's Test
The lead robber approaches Bruce. "You know, I've always wondered... the mask code says we can't prove identities, but what if..." He points his gun at Bruce's leg. "What happens if I shoot you in the kneecap?"
💭 "He's testing me. If I flinch, if I beg, the illusion breaks. Stay strong. For Baibhav's family."
Tension: MAXIMUM
3:04 PM
The Moment of Truth
Bruce stands perfectly still, maintaining eye contact. "The mask code protects everyone - heroes and villains alike. You really want to be the one who breaks it?" His voice is steady despite his racing heart.
💭 "Please believe the bluff. Please don't shoot. I have no healing factor. This will actually cripple me."
Tension: SUBSIDING
3:06 PM
Criminal Backs Down
After a tense moment, the robber lowers his gun. "You're right. The code protects us all." He moves away, but not before whispering, "We both know what you are, 'Bruce.'"
💭 "Another day, another near-death experience. How many more times can I get lucky?"
Tension: INTERNAL
3:15 PM
Police Arrive
As sirens approach, the robbers flee. The hostages are safe, but many look at Bruce with disappointment. The child's mother pulls him away, muttering about "coward heroes."
💭 "They think I failed them. Maybe I did. But 20+ innocent lives remain protected because I maintained the lie."
Tension: MEDIA
3:25 PM
Reporter Arrives
Terri Green appears with her camera crew. "Mr. Kent! Care to comment on why you didn't intervene during the robbery? Some witnesses say you could have stopped it."
💭 "And now the media circus begins. Time to play the cowardly playboy again."
Tension: AFTERMATH
4:00 PM
Alone Again
Bruce returns to his empty apartment. No one to talk to about the fear he felt. No one to share the burden with. Just another day of being a "coward" to protect others.
💭 "The real Mongoose calls me a hero. But heroes aren't supposed to feel this alone."

The Cycle Continues

This is Bruce Kent's reality: Constant danger without powers. Public scorn for "cowardice." Complete isolation to maintain the deception. Every public appearance risks exposure, injury, or death.

Yet he continues, because 20+ innocent lives depend on his sacrifice.

"The greater hero is usually the one who's less immune to bullets."
